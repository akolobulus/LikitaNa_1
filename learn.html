Health Tips

Proper Handwashing Technique

Wash for at least 20 seconds with soap and water to prevent the spread of germs.

Read more →
×

Handwashing is one of the most effective ways to prevent the spread of pathogens. Scrub all areasincluding between fingers, backs of hands, and under nails for at least 20 seconds. Rinse thoroughly with warm water and dry with a clean towel.

Importance of Vaccination

Vaccines are essential for preventing serious infectious diseases and ensuring public health.

Read more →
×

Vaccines train your immune system to recognize and fight infections safely. Completing the recommended schedule protects you and your community from outbreaks of measles, polio, hepatitis, and more.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to support body functions.

Read more →
×

Adequate hydration supports digestion, circulation, temperature regulation, and joint health. Aim for 2–3 liters of water per day, more if active or in hot weather.
