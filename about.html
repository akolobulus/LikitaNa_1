Your Padi for Good Health

Mission

LikitaNa is an AI-powered health assistant designed to deliver basic medical guidance, symptom checking, and health education in five Nigerian languages - English, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, and Pidgin. Our mission is to empower underserved and rural communities by providing accessible, understandable, and culturally relevant health information.

Key Features

Multilingual Support

Health information available in 5 Nigerian languages

Symptom Checking

AI-powered symptom analysis and guidance

Health Center Finder

Geolocation-based health facility recommendations

Health Education

Daily health tips and educational content

Progress Tracking

Achievements and health journey monitoring
