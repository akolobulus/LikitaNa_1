About LikitaNa

LikitaNa is a multilingual health assistant designed for Nigerians. We provide accessible healthcare guidance in English, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, and Pidgin.

Our mission is to empower everyone with reliable health information, regardless of language barriers or location.
